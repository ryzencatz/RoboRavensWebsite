We are FTC Team 7159, an entirely student-run FIRST® Tech Challenge team from Canyon Crest Academy. FIRST® Tech Challenge or FTC is a student robotics competition held by FIRST®. Our team has been running for about 11 years, built 15 robots, and has making an international impact on STEM education for the past 7 years. All of this has been done without ever mandating a fee for participation on the team.

For the Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology or FIRST® is an international organization that runs robotics programs for students of all ages. FIRST prepares young people for the future through a suite of life-changing youth robotics programs that build skills, confidence, and resilience. FTC or FIRST® Tech Challenge is a robotics competition which teams compete in an alliance format using robots build from a standardized set of parts. Teams design their robot to fulfill the tasks needed to be completed by the competition

Taya is an FTC Robot built by Team 7159 using the goBILDA build kit. It is designed for success in FIRST® Tech Challenge's Into the Deep season and is capable of reaching heights of about 4 feet with its linear slides. It is a powerful and agile robot that is capable of performing a variety of tasks. Taya is a reliable and sturdy robot that is perfect for our team participating in the FTC Into the Deep season.
